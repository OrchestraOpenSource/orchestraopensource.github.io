    Orchestra Open Source

    Orchestra Open Source is the organization behind a couple of 100% Open Source projects. We are a new organizations, at the moment a one man organization. All we want is to make good software that just works.

    Philosophy/Objectives:

    • KISS - Keep It Simple Stupid.
    • Less is More.
    • Sacrifice features in order to have better performance and less bugs.

    Our software guidelines/rules are:

    1. Keep the software and code as simple and small as possible.
    2. Do not create/implement new features till you get all bugs fixed.
    3. Test, test, test, test and test a new feature before making a stable version, stable means stable! Stable is something that just works.
    4. More guidelines to come.
